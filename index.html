MEANING

RED COLOR

- represents the sacrifices and blood of Egyptian martyrs.

WHITE COLOR

- refers to the purity and serenity of Egyptians.

BLACK COLOR

- refers to ages of backwardness and colonialism and that is gone forever. It also
stands for the dark period of occupation

THE GOLDEN EAGLE

- represents strength and power

WHERE IS EGYPT?

‎

Egypt is located on the northeast corner of the African continent.

It is bordered by Libya to the west,
Sudan to the south, the Red Sea to the
east, and the Mediterranean Sea to the north.
